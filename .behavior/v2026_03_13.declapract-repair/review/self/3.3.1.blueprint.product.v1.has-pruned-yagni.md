### question: do we need both stones AND guards as separate files?

could we inline guards into stones?

**analysis**: stones are instructions. guards are validation criteria. separate files allow:
- route.drive to display stone without guard
- guard evaluation to be independent
- clear separation of concerns

the wish explicitly mentions both `.stone` and `.guard` files. this is not YAGNI — it's the requested format.

### question: do we need test case [case5] route extant?

the wish says same-day invocations should reuse same route. this test verifies that findsert behavior.

**analysis**: without this test, we might not catch bugs where duplicate routes are created. test is minimal and valuable.

**analysis**: wish says `.route/v$isodate.declapract.upgrade/`. the format follows behavior route convention. no simpler alternative that maintains consistency.

**decision**: templates are marginally better for readability and follow the pattern of how init.behavior works. not YAGNI — reasonable organization.
