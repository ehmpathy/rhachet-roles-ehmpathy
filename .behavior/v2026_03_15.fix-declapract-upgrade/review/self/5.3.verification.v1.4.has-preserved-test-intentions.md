## what this teaches

when requirements change:
1. document the change in wish/vision
2. get approval (wisher sign-off on vision)
3. remove old tests that verify old behavior
4. add new tests that verify new behavior
5. articulate in review why removal is acceptable

I followed this path. the gate holds.
