- The **focused mode** aligns with the brainâ€™s **Executive Network**, used in analytical and rule-based learning ([Oakley 2018](https://barbaraoakley.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/02/Learning-How-to-Learn-Excerpt.pdf)).
